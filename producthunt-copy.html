Maker Comment
Hey Product Hunt 👋 I built HawkLeads because I kept hearing the same thing from service businesses: "We get plenty of leads. We just don't know which ones are real." A plumber gets four form submissions. One is a $15k bathroom remodel. One is a leaky faucet. They look identical in the inbox. So the team calls them in order and wastes half their day on jobs that go nowhere. HawkLeads replaces the contact form with a short qualifying flow. Visitors answer 2-5 questions (budget, timeline, what they need) and we track engagement on the site. Two signals produce one score: 0-100. Hot, warm, or cold. What makes this different from other lead scoring tools: - You see the math. Every score shows exactly why: which answers, which weights. - No black box. You set the weights. You control what "hot" means. - Works when tracking is blocked. If cookies are off, it scores on form answers alone and tells you. - Nothing is hidden. Every lead stays visible regardless of score. Scoring sorts your list, it doesn't delete anyone. The widget is under 25KB, loads async, and sits inside a Shadow DOM so it can't break your site. 30-day free trial, no credit card. Would love your feedback on the approach.
